Jaipur

Also know as the Pink City

Jaipur is a city in Rajasthan, founded in 1727. A city known for many things like the color of its building, tourist sites, and arts and craft.

Places to visit in Jaipur:

  • Hawa Mahal

  • Amber Palace

  • Jal Mahal

  • Jaigarh Fort
